What is Shush?

Shush is not about silence. It's about softening the noise — the fear, the uncertainty, the helplessness — and replacing it with something warm.

We believe comfort is a form of care. So we wrap people in softness when the world feels sharp — clothing designed to hold someone gently through the hardest time of their life.
