The Shush Story

We started with a question no one could answer.

"What do you give someone going through the hardest time of their life?"

Not flowers that wilt. Not platitudes that ring hollow. Not the awkward silence of not knowing what to say.

We asked this question when someone we loved was diagnosed. And we couldn't find a good answer anywhere.

So we created one.

Why "Shush"?

Shush isn't about silence. It's about softening the noise.

The noise of fear. The noise of uncertainty. The noise of everyone telling you to "stay strong" when you just want to cry.

Shush says: it's okay. You don't have to be strong right now. Let us wrap you in something gentle.
